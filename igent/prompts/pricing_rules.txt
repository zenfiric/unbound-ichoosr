The goal is to ensure that all datapoints that affect the pricing of the items on the registration are acounted for and included in the JSON.

+ When determining the correct product price, you need to:
 - Look for the product price information of the matched supplier.
 - Look for the number of batteries and/or number of panels that are found in the registration data and always use these numbers when determining the price.
 - Look for the correct payment type from the registration data and retrieve the right product price based on this.
 - Based on the matched supplier, payment type, number of batteries and/or number of panels, return the correct product price(s).
